Insurance premium charges prediction Project Objectives:-

The Insurance Premium Charges Prediction project aims to predict the cost of insurance premiums for individuals based on various factors such as age, gender, BMI (Body Mass Index), smoking status, number of children, and geographical region. By creating a machine learning model, the project helps insurance companies assess the potential risks associated with an individual and estimate the premium charges accurately..



Project Motivation:

Insurance companies face challenges in determining the appropriate premium for customers

to the variety of personal factors that influence the risk and cost of insurance. By

analyzing historical data, we can predict future premiums using machine learning, thus

accuracy and ensuring fair pricing for both the customer and the insurer.

It involves a contract (known as an insurance policy) between an individual or entity

(the policyholder) and an insurance company.


PROJECT GOALS:-

The goal of this project is to predict the premium charges for insurance policies based on various factors such as age, gender, BMI, number of dependents, smoking habits, and geographical region. This project leverages machine learning techniques to create a predictive model that helps insurance companies estimate the risk associated with a particular individual and thus, calculate the appropriate premium amount.
